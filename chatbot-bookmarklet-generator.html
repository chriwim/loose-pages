// How it works
01 — Configure
Choose ChatGPT or Claude, name your bookmarklet, and write your custom prompt. Use [URL] to inject the current page's URL.
02 — Install
Drag the generated bookmarklet to your browser's bookmarks bar. No extensions or sign-in needed here.
03 — Use
Navigate to any page and click your bookmark. Your chosen AI opens with your prompt and the URL, ready to go.
Bookmarklets run entirely in your browser — no data is sent anywhere except the AI you choose when you click.
